Anna

Brittany Roberts

 

Anna is an adventurous girl with a kind heart and single-minded determination. She is naive about the world and sees the good in everyone, which blinds her to their faults. Though strong and courageous, Anna is also quite stubborn and a bit clumsy.

 

Brittany has had an interest in performing since she was young. Taking every opportunity to be on the stage, acting in elementary school musicals, playing in the school band and taking drama in high school. Whether she’s singing, acting, dancing or playing a musical instrument, she is always happiest performing for an audience.

Sven

Robert Piggott

 

A unicorn among unicorns. A being so majestical that Voldemort himself has spent many nights trying to suck him.  But still Sven moves ever forward with the help of his best friend Kristoff or whoever he can find.

 

Robert is a fourth year student in biomedical and mechanical engineering who has enjoyed acting in many theatre productions over the years including two of the past engineering musicals.  When not in class he enjoys rock climbing, cooking and making sweet passionate love or reading.

 

Kristoff

Brady Snyder

 

Kristoff is a graduated engineering student who has been around Carleton for a while longer than he lets on. He spends most of his time reflecting on his glory days in Ollie's with his unlikely unicorn friend Sven.

 

Brady is a fourth year Engineering Physics student. He has had a role in the production of the Carleton Engineering Musical since its beginning in his first year at Carleton, when he played Scrap Staff.

 

Olaf

Ron Milner

 

Little is known about the mysterious Olaf, outside of the fact that he is handsome, intelligent and charismatic. Built like a fighter pilot, and armed with a grin that could melt a ceramic chastity belt, it is said that Olaf once sustained 69 Gs in the bedroom - but I'm sure you'd like to find out for yourself.

 

Ron Milner has made appearances in two other Eng Musicals. Once as "Eddy Current" in 2012's CEng Horror Picture Show, and again in 2013 as "Joey Knappa" (WD-40). Ron enjoys long walks on the beach with strong Rum, and is currently in a relationship with his Motorcycle - "Harley".

 

G.A.B.E.

Adrian Soble

 

G.A.B.E. the AI is a mysterious being heard but not seen in the hidden halls of Lobe. G.A.B.E. is a loyal, dependable artificial intelligence who will help his master when help is needed, but not without his a slightly strange humor.

 

Adrian is an Ottawa native and has spent several years attending, volunteering and working at OYP Theatre School, gaining experience working on and behind stage. He is currently in his second year of Carleton's Architectural Conservation Sustainability Engineering program.

 

The Duke

Colin Smith

 

The Duke loves to laugh evilly regarding all his diabolical plans to take over the world. He first got his taste of power when he became the acting dean of Carleton’s Faculty of Engineering, and he hasn’t looked back since.

 

Colin is in his third year of Biomedical and Mechanical Engineering, at Carleton. Volunteering at Leo’s Lounge and the Carleton Student Engineering Society, he loves being involved. This will be Colin's third year  with the Carleton Engineering musical and he thrilled to be involved once again.

Hans

Noah Pacheco

 

Hans is a cocky upper-year engineer that cares about only three things:  maintaining his muscular physique, putting his penis in things and preventing any form of arts from taking place in his school.

 

Noah Pacheco is a first year Aerospace Engineering student. His theatrical experience originates from a young age. He has been in multiple productions since then; the largest being last year's premier of the production "Break Before Bend" by Elliot Wehrle.
